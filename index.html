Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes, absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. We never store or transmit your files to any server.

How does the compression work?

We use advanced algorithms to optimize PDF content while maintaining quality. This includes image optimization, font subsetting, and structure optimization.

What's the maximum file size?

Free users can process files up to 5MB, Pro users up to 100MB, and Enterprise users have no limits.

Can I process multiple files at once?

Yes, Pro and Enterprise users can process multiple files simultaneously. Free users are limited to one file per day.
